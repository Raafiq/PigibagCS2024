about us

Finding it hard to obtain your favourite products from foreign lands? Don’t want to wait FOREVER to unbox that special item you’ve been waiting for? You’ve come to the right place.

Pigibag, a unique peer-to-peer personal shopping experience, was founded in October 2017 to connect shoppers and travelers together to benefit all.

We pair shoppers with the ever growing and present network of travelers to make popular brands and unique items from all over the world easily accessible to the everyday consumer.

No more pricy overseas shipping costs, no more waiting for your friend to visit in order to enjoy your beloved products.
