Pigibag aims to provide a convenient and safe method of purchase and delivery, doing it outside of Pigibag puts you at risk.

By communicating and purchasing via Pigibag, you will have access to our secure payment gateway, purchase status tracking service and Pigibag 24/7 online customer support.

Your "Willing to Pay" price should cover some tips to the traveller on top of the cost price of the item. You are encouraged to give about $5 tips for small and easy to get items, while bulkier or difficult to get items should offer a higher tip.

Note that the higher the amount the requester is willing to pay on top of retail price, the faster and more likely the request will get picked up!

When you accept a purchase offer from the traveller, the amount is deducted from your Pigibag e-wallet in Pigibag and will only be released to the traveller after you confirmed the delivery.

Pigibag accepts the following forms of payment: Bank Transfer, PayPal, e-NETS, Credit Cards

Requesters will not be able to cancel the transaction after the deposit is placed. However, travellers may still cancel the request in the event the requested items cannot be found, is out of stock, etc.

This is to ensure that travellers may proceed with the purchases without fear of requesters backing out at the last minute, or after the items have been bought.

There are several ways you can identify a trustworthy traveller:


Status: Recommended Travellers are users who have attained multiple 4 and 5-star reviews on Pigibag.

Delivery Rate: This represents the likelihood of the traveller completing your request after offering, based on their past transactions.

Reviews: Check out what previous requesters of this traveller have to say about him/her.
